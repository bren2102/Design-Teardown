Person Of The Week

Simon Collison is a designer, writer, and speaker from Nottingham. An early web standards advocate, he has twenty years experience leading design at companies such as Fictive Kin, Erskine, and Agenzia and assisting governments, banks, airlines, tech giants, record labels, publishers, and charities. To connect the community, Simon organizes the New Adventures conference, a future-focused event with a focus on critical design, inclusivity, and sustainability. You can find him on Twitter as @colly. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Simon!
